Yummy Brie-Stuffed Mushroom Caps!

Roasted with a slathering of garlic-thyme butter, these simple Brie-stuffed mushrooms make an irresistible appetizer thats sure to get any party started.

Appetizer

Serves 6-8 People

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup (60 mL) Ontario butter, melted.
  • 2 tbsp (30 mL) finely chopped fresh thyme.
  • 1 tbsp (15 mL) Dijon mustard.
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced.
  • 1/4 tsp (1 mL) salt.
  • 1/4 tsp (1 mL) black pepper.
  • 24 medium mushrooms, stemmed.
  • 8 oz (125 g) Ontario Brie wheel, cut into 24 chunks.
  • 1/2 cup (125 mL) finely chopped toasted pecan.s
  • 2 tbsp (30 mL) finely chopped fresh chives.
  • 2 tbsp (60 mL) liquid honey.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In small bowl, stir together melted butter, thyme, Dijon, garlic, salt and pepper.
  3. Arrange mushrooms, cavity-side up, on parchment paper–lined baking sheet. Brush mushrooms with garlic butter.
  4. Roast for 15 to 20 minutes or until tender.
  5. Arrange a piece of Brie into each mushroom with piece of Brie. Sprinkle with pecans and chives, and drizzle with honey.
  6. Roast for 4 to 5 minutes or until cheese has melted. Serve hot.
  7. Substitute Ontario Brie with Ontario feta cheese if preferred.
